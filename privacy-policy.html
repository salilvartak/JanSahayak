Privacy Policy

JanSahayak — Visual Annotation Assistant  |  Effective: 6 April 2026

1. What JanSahayak Does

JanSahayak helps users understand their surroundings by analysing photos with AI. You point your camera, ask a question, and receive a spoken explanation.

2. Information We Collect

  • Camera images — Photos you take inside the app are sent to our server for AI analysis. We store original and annotated images to provide the service and improve accuracy.
  • Microphone audio — Voice recordings are sent to our server for speech-to-text transcription. Audio is processed in real time and is not stored after transcription.
  • Device identifier — A randomly generated ID is stored on your device to associate your conversation history. This is not linked to your personal identity.
  • Queries and responses — Your text questions and the AI-generated answers are stored to enable conversation history and improve the service.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To analyse images and provide visual assistance
  • To transcribe your voice queries
  • To maintain your conversation history
  • To improve the accuracy and quality of the service

4. Permissions We Request

  • Camera — Required to capture photos for visual analysis.
  • Microphone — Required for voice input so you can ask questions by speaking.
  • Internet — Required to send images and queries to our AI backend for processing.
  • Storage (Android 12 and below) — Required to pick images from your gallery.

5. Third-Party Services

JanSahayak uses the following third-party services to function:

  • Google Gemini API — For image analysis, object detection, and natural language explanation. Images and queries are sent to Google's servers. See Google AI Terms.
  • Microsoft Azure — For hosting our backend and speech-to-text services. See Microsoft Privacy Statement.
  • Supabase — For image storage and database. See Supabase Privacy Policy.
  • Neo4j Aura — For knowledge graph storage. See Neo4j Privacy Policy.

6. Data Retention

Conversation history and images are retained for as long as you use the app. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

7. Data Security

All communication between the app and our servers uses HTTPS encryption. We take reasonable measures to protect your data, but no system is 100% secure.

8. Children's Privacy

JanSahayak is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
